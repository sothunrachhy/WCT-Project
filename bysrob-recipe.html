Bai Sach Chrouk (Cambodian Grilled Pork & Rice)

A beloved Cambodian breakfast of smoky coconut-marinated pork served over broken jasmine rice with fresh pickled vegetables and a light broth.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Pork

instructions

step 1

Marinate the pork: In a bowl, combine 0.5 cups coconut milk, 6 garlic cloves (minced), 3 tablespoons fish sauce, 2 tablespoons oyster sauce, 2 tablespoons sugar, and 1 teaspoon black pepper. Add the 500 grams pork shoulder or loin (thinly sliced) and mix well to coat. Cover and refrigerate.

step 2

Make the pickled vegetables: Mix 4 tablespoons white vinegar, 1 tablespoon sugar, and 0.5 teaspoons salt until dissolved. Toss in the 150 grams daikon radish (julienned) and 100 grams carrots (julienned). Set aside to pickle while you prepare everything else.

step 3

Cook the rice: Rinse 2 cups jasmine rice (preferably broken rice) until water runs clear. Add to a pot with 3 cups water, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until cooked.

step 4

Warm the broth: Gently heat 2 cups chicken broth in a small pot with 3 slices of fresh ginger. Season with a splash of fish sauce to taste. Keep warm on low heat. Garnish with 3 spring onions (sliced) before serving.

step 5

Grill the pork: Heat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the marinated pork slices in batches, 2–5 minutes per side, until nicely charred and cooked through.

step 6

Rest the pork: Remove pork from the grill and let it rest briefly before slicing into bite-sized pieces.

step 7

Assemble & serve: Serve a generous scoop of rice in a bowl, top with sliced grilled pork and a heap of pickled vegetables. Pour a small cup of warm ginger broth on the side. Enjoy immediately!

ingredients

500 g pork shoulder or loin, thinly sliced

0.5 cups coconut milk

6 garlic cloves, minced

3 tablespoons fish sauce

2 tablespoons oyster sauce

2 tablespoons sugar

1 teaspoon black pepper

2 cups jasmine rice (preferably broken rice)

3 cups water (for rice)

150 g daikon radish, julienned

100 g carrots, julienned

4 tablespoons white vinegar

1 tablespoon sugar (for pickles)

0.5 teaspoons salt (for pickles)

2 cups chicken broth

3 spring onions, sliced

3 slices fresh ginger

tools

Grill or Grill Pan

Mixing Bowl

Knife & Cutting Board

Rice Pot
