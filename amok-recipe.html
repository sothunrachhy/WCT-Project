Cambodian Fish Amok

Fish Amok is one of the dishes I return to again and again—not because it’s famous, but because it holds the soul of Khmer cooking. The curry is built on kroeung: lemongrass, galangal, turmeric, and kaffir lime, pounded until fragrant and alive. Then we bring in coconut cream, herbs, and a gentle set from egg, steaming everything until the texture becomes silky—almost like a savory custard. The result is calm, balanced, and deeply Cambodian. Fish Amok isn’t just a dish; it’s a story we carry forward through taste and care.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4-6 servings

Tags : Fish

instructions

step 1

Prepare all the Kreoung Past ready with the right amount.

step 2

Heat the pan and pour the vegetable oil and wait for it to warm, then put kaffir lime leaves, shrimp past, dried chilies past and all Kreoung Past together to fry until it smells good.

step 3

Put fish fillet in and other ingredients, fry them until the fish is cooked.

step 4

Pour coconut milk and taste to your preference, then it's done.

step 5

Have the banana leaf wrapped with star gooseberry leaf placed inside, then add the ready Amok in.

step 6

Add the cooked coconut milk on top of the Amok and make some decoration with sliced lemongrass and chilies.

step 7

Amok can be served with Jasmine rice.

Note: The original Amok Fish has all the ingredients mixed with fish and put in a young coconut shell or wrapped in banana leaf to be steamed, with egg added on top.

ingredients

0.25kg fish fillet (any fish of your preference)

3 tbsp lemongrass past (Kreoung Past), 180gr lemon grass slice, 35gr garlic, 16gr galangal, 10gr turmeric, 10gr finger root, 1gr kaffir lime leaf

½ tbsp dried chilies past, 1/3 tbsp shrimp past / Prahok (based on preference), 3 tbsp vegetable oil, 1 tbsp fish sauce, 1 tbsp palm sugar, 1 pinch of salt

70ml coconut cream/milk, 125ml chicken soup

½ Kaffir Lime Leaves for garnish, Banana leaf for wrapping (optional), star gooseberry leaf, Steamed Jasmine rice (optional)

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
