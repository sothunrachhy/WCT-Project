Kola Noodle

A refreshing Cambodian noodle salad served with shredded vegetables, dried shrimps, roasted peanuts, and a tangy Sweet & Sour Fish Sauce dressing known as Teuk Trey Pa-em.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2-4 servings

Tags : noodle

instructions

step 1

Simmer the rice noodle until soft and ready to eat. Set aside. Crush the peanuts and keep them together with the dried shrimps.

step 2

Boil eggs for 10 mins. Wait until cold, then peel and set aside.

step 3

Chop garlic into small pieces. Heat the frying pan, add a few drops of vegetable oil, then stir in the garlic slowly until golden brown. Pour into the noodle bowl, mix well with dark soy sauce, then add the shredded vegetables and toss for nice attractive colours.

step 4

Make the Sweet & Sour Fish Sauce (Teuk Trey Pa-em): bring water to a boil, add minced garlic, then season with sugar, salt, fish sauce, and lime juice or vinegar. Taste and adjust. Add chili flakes as desired.

step 5

Serve the noodles with shredded vegetables, peanuts, dried shrimps, pickles, and hard-boiled egg. Drizzle Teuk Trey Pa-em as the dressing sauce.

ingredients

Rice noodle

Pickles

Shredded green papaya, shredded cucumber, shredded carrot

Bean sprout

Mixed fresh Khmer basil leaves

Dark soy sauce, cooking oil

Dried shrimps, ground roast peanut

Hard-boiled egg

Chopped garlic, chili flakes

Teuk Trey Pa-em: lime juice / vinegar, sugar, salt, fish sauce, boiled water, garlic
