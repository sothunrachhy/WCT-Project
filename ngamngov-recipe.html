Lemongrass Soup

Tasty chicken soup

prep time

5 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

4-5 servings

Tags : Chicken

instructions

step 1

In a pot, boil water with garlic, onion, kaffir leaves and lemongrass powder for 5 minutes.

step 2

Add chicken and let it boil for 5 more minutes, then turn heat down to low and let simmer for 15 more minutes.

step 3

Season with fish sauce, sugar and salt. Garnish with basil and a squeeze of lime. Serve with steamed rice.

ingredients

4 tsp Senteurs d'Angkor Lemongrass powder

500 g chicken

water (enough to submerge the chicken)

2 tbsp fish sauce

2 tbsp sugar

3 tsp salt

1 clove of garlic (chopped)

1 medium onion (sliced)

3-4 kaffir leaves

fresh basil (chopped)

3 green limes

tools

Frying Pan

Mixing Bowl

Knife & Cutting Board

Measuring Spoons
